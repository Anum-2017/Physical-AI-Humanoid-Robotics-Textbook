## Implementation Notes

To use this navigation in your chapter:
1. Add the component after the main heading
2. Update the previous/next links to match your chapter sequence
3. Ensure section IDs match the links in the jump menu
4. Verify all links point to existing content

```mdx
---
title: Your Chapter Title
---

# Your Chapter Title

import Navigation from '@site/src/components/navigation.mdx';

<Navigation />
```
